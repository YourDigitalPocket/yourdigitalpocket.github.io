Factory introduction

  ↵ZOGEAR Medical Co.,Ltd

ZOGEAR Medical Co.,Ltd is professional manufacturer for medical devices, with office located in Shanghai, factory located in Yangzhou city, where is the main production base for disposable medical devices. We are a professional company integrating research and development, production and trading for medical products, mainly for dental and protective items.

Products

Products: Medical protecive face masks, disposable medical face masks, Doctor caps, face shield, isolation gowns, first aid kit, implant kit,  saliva ejectors, denture box, disposable instrument kit, irrigation needles, dental bibs, cotton rolls etc.
